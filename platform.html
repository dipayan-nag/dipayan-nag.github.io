MY PLATFORM

Primary Priorities

If elected, I will work to unite voices within the international community into three core primary objectives:

  • Lobbying to make tuition and MSP premiums affordable for international students
  • Increasing international, People of Colour (POC), and LGBTQIA+ representation in UVSS governance
  • Lobbying to introduce fair housing regulations for international students
